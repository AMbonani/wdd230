About Pretoria

Pretoria is the administrative capital of South Africa, known for its Jacaranda-lined streets and beautiful gardens.

Demographics

  • Population: 2.4 million (as of 2024)
  • Official languages: Afrikaans, English, isiNdebele, isiXhosa, isiZulu, Sepedi, Sesotho, Setswana, siSwati, Tshivenda, Xitsonga
  • Ethnic groups: Black African, White, Coloured, Indian or Asian
